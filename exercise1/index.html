Beef Noodle

Taiwanese beef noodle soup, 台灣牛肉麵 (tai wan niu rou mian), is an iconic Taiwanese dish featuring rich simmered beef broth, slow-braised stew beef and beef tendon, and hand-pulled noodles.

A variety of ingredients are used as toppings, the most common being pickled mustard greens, blanched bok choy, and a generous spoonful of chili sauce.

Bubble Tea

An incredibly unique looking beverage, Bubble tea is a Taiwanese recipe made by blending a tea base with milk, fruit and fruit juices, then adding the signature “bubbles” - yummy tapioca pearls that sit at the bottom.

Bubble tea is served in transparent cups with a fat straw so that - as you sip - the tapioca balls (also known as “pearls” or “boba”) come shooting up it and can be chewed as you swallow down the delicious liquid.

Chicken Cutlet

Crunchy alert! These Taiwanese Fried Chicken Cutlets (炸雞排) are seriously one of the best things from Taiwan! With a super crumbly and crispy coating, these chicken cutlets are deeply savory and come-in GIANT sizes.

The chicken cutlets are pound to super thin then seasoned with a special salt and pepper spice mix.
